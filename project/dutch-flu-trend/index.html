DutchFluTrend


Schneider PP1,2, van Gool CJAW3, Spreeuwenberg P4, Hooiveld M4, Donker GA4, Barnett DJ1, Paget J4.

1 Faculty of Health, Medicine and Life Sciences, Maastricht University; 2 School of Health and Related Research, University of Sheffield; 3 Nivel (Netherlands Institute of Health Service Research);
4 School CAPHRI, Maastricht University


In the first stage of the project, I investigated whether Wikipedia page views were correlated with the incidence of influenza in several countries around the world.
  • Code and data repository[Deprecated!]


Subsequently, I included Google search queries and used several statical learning techniques to estimate the incidence of influenza in Germany in near real time.
  • Conference poster for WEON 2018
  • A tutorial on digital epidemiology in R
  • Data & code repository


Finally, we piloted the method during the 2017-2018 influenza-like illness epidemic in the Netherlands.

  • Preprint: Schneider PP, van Gool CJAW, Spreeuwenberg P, Hooiveld M, Donker GA, Barnett DJ, Paget J. Preprint: Using digital epidemiology methods to monitor influenza-like illness in the Netherlands in real-time: the 2017-2018 season. bioRxiv. 2018. https://doi.org/10.1101/440867
